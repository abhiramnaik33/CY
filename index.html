Course Overview

GM University's B.Tech in Computer Science - Cybersecurity is a four-year program designed to develop skilled professionals in digital security. Accredited and globally recognized, it offers flexible learning, career enhancement, and preparation for certifications like CEH and CISSP.

With industry-aligned training, students gain hands-on experience in advanced labs, mentorship, and internships, ensuring readiness for global cybersecurity challenges.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates need 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Computer Science/Electronics, with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC from Karnataka). A valid KCET or JEE Main score is required. International students need IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 80. Lateral entry is available for Diploma holders with 50% marks.

Curriculum Highlights

The curriculum blends Computer Science fundamentals with cybersecurity practices, covering ethical hacking, cryptography, and AI-driven security. Students engage in hands-on labs, CTF competitions, and internships with top firms, preparing them for cutting-edge areas like Quantum Cryptography and Zero Trust Architecture.

Fee Structure

Annual tuition is ₹2,25,000, with a one-time admission fee of ₹25,000. Total program cost is ₹9,25,000, with flexible payment options and scholarships up to 50% for eligible students.

About the Department +

The Department of Cyber Security and Information Security at GM University, Davanagere, Karnataka, was established with the vision of fostering excellence in the rapidly evolving field of digital security. Recognizing the increasing significance of securing data, networks, and digital infrastructure in the modern world, the department is committed to equipping students with advanced technical knowledge, practical skills, and ethical perspectives to meet global cybersecurity challenges.

The department offers two specialized programs i.e Cyber Security and Information Security that blend theoretical foundations with hands-on training in areas such as cryptography, ethical hacking, digital forensics, network defense, artificial intelligence in security, and risk management. With an emphasis on research-driven learning, students are encouraged to engage in innovative projects that address real-world problems in cybercrime prevention, data protection, and secure systems design.

Supported by experienced faculty members, modern laboratories, and collaborations with industry and government organizations, the department provides a platform for holistic learning and professional development. It also actively organizes workshops, seminars, and training programs to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

By promoting skilled cybersecurity professionals, the Department of Cyber Security and Information Security at GM University plays a vital role in strengthening digital resilience and contributing to India’s vision of a secure and technology-driven future.

Vision & Mission +

Vision

Excellence and leadership in the educational, professional and research field of cyber security and Information security at the regional and global levels. Ethically capable of proactively safeguarding the digital future and commitment to effectively meet the needs of society and the labor market.

Mission

Preparing professional cadres in cybersecurity and information security in both the academic and professional fields capable of leading, designing and developing various projects in the various fields of cybersecurity and information security, by providing a distinguished and high-quality program that focuses on integrating scientific theories and practical training to develop programs and applications and innovation in scientific research and provide the required security services to individuals and public and private institutions in India and contribute to the development of society.

Program Objectives +

The Cyber Security and Information Security program is carefully structured to equip students with the necessary knowledge, technical skills, and professional values required to succeed in the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity. The program emphasizes knowledge and problem-solving by providing a strong theoretical foundation and practical expertise to analyze, assess, and address cybersecurity challenges through innovative, effective, and industry-relevant solutions. By fostering professional excellence, it prepares graduates to qualify for and excel in leading positions within cybersecurity, information security, and related technology-driven sectors, ensuring they are highly competitive in the labor market.

An important component of the program is the promotion of ethical and legal awareness, ensuring that students understand professional responsibilities, legal frameworks, and ethical practices while addressing security challenges responsibly and sustainably. The curriculum also develops teamwork and collaboration, training students to work efficiently within multidisciplinary teams while fostering leadership, communication, and interpersonal problem-solving skills essential for professional success.

To address the dynamic nature of cybersecurity, the program introduces the practice of continuous learning, enabling students to stay updated with emerging technologies, tools, and methodologies, thereby preparing them to adapt to industry shifts and advancements. Through a strong focus on research and innovation, students are encouraged to explore and contribute to advancements in areas such as ethical hacking, digital forensics, encryption, cyber warfare, cloud security, and network defense, thereby enhancing both academic and practical contributions to the field.

The program also emphasizes programming and technical competence, with particular focus on languages like Python and other cybersecurity-relevant tools that enable students to develop, test, and deploy advanced security solutions. By ensuring graduate readiness, the curriculum creates pathways for higher education, specialized research, and advanced professional training. Furthermore, it prepares graduates to provide community and industry support, contributing to the strengthening of cybersecurity infrastructure, mitigating risks, and offering expertise to national and international organizations.

Finally, the program ensures global and local relevance by aligning its curriculum with the evolving demands of the labor market and addressing both regional and global cybersecurity challenges. In doing so, it nurtures professionals capable of strengthening digital resilience and contributing meaningfully to securing the future of cyberspace.
