About the Department

The Department of Cyber Security and Information Security at GM University, Davanagere, Karnataka, is dedicated to excellence in digital security education. Our programs in Cyber Security and Information Security blend theoretical foundations with hands-on training in cryptography, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and more. With state-of-the-art labs, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, we prepare students to tackle global cybersecurity challenges and contribute to a secure digital future.

Vision & Mission

Vision – Cyber Security

Equip students to address global cybersecurity challenges, secure digital data ethically, and develop innovative security systems.

Vision – Information Security

Prepare graduates to protect information, manage secure systems, and innovate in information security solutions.

Mission – Cyber Security

Understand cybersecurity concepts, design robust tools, and apply innovative methods for secure data management.

Mission – Information Security

Apply information security principles, design reliable frameworks, and leverage emerging technologies for secure environments.

Program Highlights

Cyber Security

Our Cyber Security program equips students with skills in computer science, networking, and cryptography to combat cyber threats. With hands-on training and ethical practices, graduates are prepared to protect critical systems and pursue advanced certifications.

Information Security

The Information Security program focuses on securing data and systems through encryption, access control, and risk management. Students gain practical experience and ethical insights to lead in secure digital ecosystems.
