Message from Head of Department

Dr. Aruna Kumar B T

Associate Professor & HoD

CS-Cyber Security & Information Security

Dear Students and Aspirants, It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the Undergraduate Program in Cybersecurity and Information Security. In today's digital era, information is the most valuable asset, and securing it has become a global priority.

Our program is designed to blend strong engineering foundations with specialized knowledge in cyber defense, cryptography, digital forensics, ethical hacking, and security management. With a curriculum that is both industry-oriented and research-driven, we aim to prepare graduates who can face real-world security challenges with confidence and innovation.

At GM University, we emphasize not only technical expertise but also ethical responsibility, problem-solving skills, and lifelong learning. I invite you to join us on this exciting journey of discovery, innovation, and excellence in Cyber and Information Security.

About the Department

Leading the future of security-driven innovation

The Department of CS-Cyber Security and Department of CS-Information Security at GM University is at the forefront of network analytics, network security, and topologies, dedicated to advancing the field of cybersecurity through innovative research and quality education. Established in 2024, our department has quickly become a center of excellence for cybersecurity education and research.

We offer comprehensive programs that blend theoretical foundations with practical applications, preparing students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity. Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure relevance and employability.

Vision, Mission & Objectives

Our guiding principles for academic excellence

Vision

The Department of CS-Cyber Security and CS-Information Security will have a transformative impact on society through continual innovation in cybersecurity education, research, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and secure software development — fostering creativity, skill development, and entrepreneurship.

Mission

  • Understand cybersecurity concepts in real-time applications and problem-solving.
  • Design robust tools and applications for secure data management.
  • Foster research, entrepreneurship, and industry partnerships.
  • Empower students with digital leadership skills and global perspectives.
  • Apply innovative methods for digital security in automation.

Objectives

  • Equip students with skills to meet global-level cybersecurity challenges.
  • Empower graduates to secure digital data while upholding ethical values.
  • Build professionals capable of developing automated and innovative digital security systems.

Programs & Courses

Cutting-edge curricula designed for the cybersecurity workforce

B.Tech CS-Cyber Security

The Cyber Security program prepares graduates with knowledge, skills, and professional values required to address complex challenges of securing digital infrastructure. With a strong foundation in computer science, networking, and cryptography, students identify vulnerabilities, mitigate risks, and develop robust security solutions. Emphasis is placed on hands-on training, industry-relevant tools, and real-world attack and defense mechanisms.

B.Tech CS-Information Security

The Information Security program develops graduates with expertise in securing data, applications, and communication systems. It provides balance of theoretical foundations and practical skills in encryption, access control, compliance, governance, and risk management. Students gain hands-on experience with advanced tools, enabling them to design and implement effective strategies for data protection and trust assurance.
